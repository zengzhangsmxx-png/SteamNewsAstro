Grand Theft Auto V Enhanced offers newcomers an overwhelming sandbox of activities, missions, and mechanics. This comprehensive guide provides essential strategies for mastering the game's core systems, maximizing your earnings, and avoiding common beginner mistakes. Whether you're new to the GTA series or returning after years away, these tips will accelerate your progress through Los Santos and Blaine County.

The Enhanced edition includes improved graphics, faster loading times, and quality-of-life improvements that make it the definitive way to experience Rockstar's crime epic. With over 190 million copies sold worldwide and maintaining consistent player engagement on Steam, GTA V continues attracting new players seeking guidance through its complex systems.

## Character Management and Switching

The three-protagonist system forms GTA V's core gameplay loop. Franklin Clinton excels at driving and serves as your entry point into the criminal underworld. Michael De Santa brings heist planning expertise and connections, while Trevor Philips offers raw firepower and access to unique missions.

Master the character switching mechanic early by using the character wheel (Alt key on PC). Each protagonist has distinct special abilities: Franklin's driving focus slows time during vehicle sequences, Michael's bullet time enhances combat accuracy, and Trevor's rampage mode increases damage output while reducing incoming damage.

Character stats improve through gameplay activities. Driving skill increases through time spent behind the wheel, shooting improves at firing ranges, and stamina builds through running and cycling. Focus on developing each character's strengths rather than trying to balance all stats equally.

## Essential Controls and Movement

GTA V Enhanced features refined controls compared to previous entries. Walking uses WASD keys, while holding Left Shift enables running. The cover system activates with Right Mouse Button near walls or objects, providing protection during firefights.

Vehicle controls vary by type but follow consistent patterns. Cars use standard WASD movement with Space for handbrake and Left Shift for acceleration boost when available. Aircraft require more practice, with Num 8/5 controlling pitch and Num 4/6 managing roll.

Combat mechanics reward precision over spray-and-pray tactics. Aim with Right Mouse Button and fire with Left Mouse Button. The auto-aim system helps beginners, but manual aiming provides better accuracy for headshots and distant targets.

## Money-Making Strategies for Beginners

Early game finances require careful management. Story missions provide guaranteed income ranging from $5,000 to $25,000 per completion, making them your primary revenue source initially. Avoid expensive purchases like supercars or aircraft until establishing steady income streams.

The stock market system offers massive profit potential through Lester's assassination missions. Complete "The Hotel Assassination" as required, but save remaining assassination missions until after the main story finale. This strategy allows investing all three characters' money for maximum returns.

Property investments become viable after accumulating $10+ million per character. The Los Santos Customs shops generate $1,600 weekly, while the Downtown Cab Co. provides $2,000 weekly income. These investments pay for themselves within 50-60 game weeks.

## Mission Structure and Progression

Story missions unlock through character-specific markers on the map. Yellow markers indicate main story content, while blue markers represent strangers and freaks side missions. Prioritize story missions to unlock new areas, weapons, and gameplay mechanics.

Heist missions represent GTA V's signature content, requiring careful crew selection and approach planning. Choose experienced crew members for better success rates, even though they demand higher cuts. The improved performance often justifies the additional cost through bonus objectives and reduced failure rates.

Mission replay functionality allows improving scores and experimenting with different approaches. Gold medal requirements challenge experienced players but aren't necessary for story progression. Focus on completion first, then return for perfectionist runs.

## Combat and Weapon Systems

Weapon variety spans pistols, assault rifles, explosives, and melee options. Each category serves specific purposes: pistols for stealth, rifles for sustained firefights, and explosives for vehicle destruction. Purchase weapons from Ammu-Nation stores and customize them with attachments for improved performance.

Armor provides crucial protection during intense firefights. Body armor absorbs damage before affecting health, making it essential for difficult missions. Snacks restore health during combat, accessible through the interaction menu without pausing gameplay.

Stealth mechanics reward patience and planning. Crouching reduces noise and visibility, while silenced weapons eliminate enemies without alerting others. Many missions offer stealth approaches that reduce overall difficulty and provide bonus rewards.

## Vehicle Mechanics and Customization

Los Santos features hundreds of vehicle types across multiple categories. Sports cars offer speed but poor durability, while SUVs provide protection at the cost of handling. Motorcycles enable quick navigation through traffic but leave riders vulnerable to damage.

Los Santos Customs shops allow extensive vehicle modification. Performance upgrades improve acceleration, top speed, and handling characteristics. Visual customizations include paint jobs, wheels, and body modifications that don't affect performance but allow personal expression.

Vehicle storage works differently for each character. Personal vehicles can be saved in character-specific garages, but stolen vehicles disappear when switching characters or reloading saves. Purchase important vehicles rather than relying on theft for long-term transportation.

## Exploration and Side Activities

Los Santos and Blaine County contain numerous optional activities beyond main missions. Golf, tennis, and darts provide relaxation between intense story sequences while building character relationships and stats.

Collectibles scattered throughout the world offer completion rewards and exploration incentives. Letter scraps unlock a unique outfit, while spaceship parts provide access to special vehicles. These activities extend gameplay significantly beyond the main story's 30-hour duration.

Random events occur throughout the world, presenting opportunities for quick cash or unique encounters. Armored car robberies yield $5,000-$8,000, while helping stranded motorists sometimes leads to stock tips or mission unlocks.

## Technical Optimization Tips

GTA V Enhanced benefits from proper graphics settings optimization. The game includes extensive options for balancing visual quality with performance. Start with medium settings and adjust individual options based on your hardware capabilities.

Save frequently using the quick save option in the pause menu. The game's autosave system works reliably, but manual saves prevent progress loss during experimental gameplay or risky activities.

Loading times vary significantly between storage types. SSD installation reduces loading times by 40-60% compared to traditional hard drives, particularly noticeable when switching characters or fast traveling.

## Conclusion

Success in Grand Theft Auto V Enhanced comes from understanding its interconnected systems and planning your approach strategically. Focus on story progression first, save money for post-game investments, and experiment with the game's vast array of activities at your own pace.
