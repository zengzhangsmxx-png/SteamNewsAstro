The update, officially designated as version 1.4.2, delivers enhanced combat systems, improved NPC artificial intelligence, and performance optimizations that have boosted the game's Steam review score to 87% positive from 82% positive last month. The most significant change in the latest update involves a complete overhaul of the combat mechanics that originally drew criticism from players and reviewers. Key combat improvements include:

- Reduced stamina consumption during defensive maneuvers by 20%
- Enhanced parry timing windows for improved responsiveness
- Streamlined combo system with clearer visual feedback
- Improved mounted combat mechanics with better horse AI
