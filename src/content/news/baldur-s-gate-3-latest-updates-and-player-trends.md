Larian Studios has released Patch 7 for Baldur's Gate 3, introducing official mod support through Steam Workshop integration and expanding the game's conclusion with 12 new ending variations. The mod support system allows players to browse, install, and manage community-created content directly through the game's main menu, eliminating the need for third-party mod managers.

## Expanded Endings Address Player Feedback

Patch 7 introduces 12 additional ending sequences that respond to specific character choices and relationship combinations previously overlooked. These additions focus on companion storylines and provide closure for romance paths that felt incomplete in the original release. The new content totals approximately 90 minutes of additional dialogue and cutscenes.

Larian's narrative team worked with the original voice cast to record new lines, maintaining consistency with the established performances. The expanded endings trigger based on specific decision trees tracked throughout the campaign, requiring players to meet precise criteria for access.

## Technical Improvements and Bug Fixes

The update resolves 127 reported issues, including a memory leak that caused performance degradation during sessions exceeding four hours. Loading times between areas have decreased by an average of 25%, with the most significant improvements occurring in Act 3's dense urban environments.

Graphics optimization reduces VRAM usage by 15% on high settings, benefiting players with 8GB graphics cards. The update also fixes texture streaming issues that caused visual pop-in during rapid camera movement.

Steam's concurrent player data indicates sustained engagement following the patch release. User review sentiment has improved, with 89% of recent reviews rating the game positively compared to 84% in the previous month.

Community forums show increased discussion around mod creation and sharing. The official Larian Discord server has gained 23,000 new members since Patch 7's announcement, with the modding channels becoming the most active discussion areas.

Larian has released comprehensive documentation for aspiring mod creators, including asset guidelines and scripting references.
